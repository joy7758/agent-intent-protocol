# AIP Boundaries

## 1. Purpose

This document records what AIP does not attempt to standardize.

## 2. Out of Scope

- transport framing
- chat transcript formatting
- persona issuance and identity attestation
- policy language or budget-rule syntax
- execution-integrity proof construction
- audit receipt packaging
- full workflow orchestration

## 3. Why the Boundaries Matter

Separating interaction semantics from adjacent layers reduces ambiguity:

- persona remains an identity concern
- governance remains a decision concern
- execution integrity remains a runtime truth concern
- audit evidence remains a review and receipt concern

## 4. Adjacent Layer Handoff

AIP is designed to hand compact semantic objects into governance, runtime, and evidence systems without replacing them.
